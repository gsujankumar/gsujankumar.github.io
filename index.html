About Me

I am currently with Amazon Web Services as a Applied Scientist. I work on Amazon CodeWhisperer, a ML based code companion for developers.

I am passionate about using the advances in ML and AI in solving real-world problems. My research interests are in generative models for code and images, model compression and inference optimizations.

Prior to joining Amazon, I graduated with a Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where I was advised by Prof. Naresh Shanbhag. I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, with a bachelor's and master's degree in Electrical Engineering. My master's thesis was on optical phase-locked loops. If you are interested in my research further check out my publications, or this book that I co-authored.
